About Delicious Bites

Bringing people together through great food and unforgettable dining experiences.

Our Story

Delicious Bites was founded in Nairobi in 2020 with the goal of serving fresh, high-quality meals in a welcoming environment. What began as a small family-owned restaurant has grown into a favourite dining destination for students, families, and professionals.

We believe that food is more than just a meal. It is an experience that brings people together and creates lasting memories.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional food and service while maintaining the highest standards of quality and hospitality.

Our Vision

To become one of Nairobi's most trusted and loved restaurants by creating memorable dining experiences for every guest.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef James Mwangi

With over 12 years of culinary experience, Chef James specializes in African and international cuisine.
